About Us

Sonoray Medical Systems is a Chennai-based ultrasound and veterinary equipment provider serving customers across India. We deliver portable scanners, console machines, probes, veterinary consumables and reliable imaging solutions.

Our business model focuses on operational excellence, affordable technology and long-term customer support. We believe in providing high-quality diagnostic equipment with professional service.

Our Mission

To offer dependable diagnostic solutions with nationwide service support and fast delivery.

Our Vision

To be India’s most reliable ultrasound equipment supplier recognised for trust and support.
